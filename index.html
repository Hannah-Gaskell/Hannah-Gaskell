Basic info

The beach is a wonderful place. A beach can have sand, stones or a mixture of both.

The sea water can be calm or stormy, and you may see and hear waves along the shoreline.

There are sometimes boats out to sea and dolphins.

Many people enjoy spending days out at the beach as it enables them to relax and enjoy their surroundings.

Things you can do at the beach!

  • Swim in the sea
  • Walk on the sand or stones
  • Listen to the waves

Some more info about beaches

Sandy beaches occupy about one third of global coastlines.

It takes thousands of years for beaches to form.They form because of the water that is moving constantly.

The waves break down the land, including rocks and coral reefs.
